About CashPrep estimates

CashPrep is a simple planning tool that focuses on the living‑expense part of college costs and intentionally leaves tuition out. It helps you think about how much spending money you may want to save for one academic year.

The estimates draw on the same ideas colleges and the U.S. Department of Education use when they build a student’s “cost of attendance,” which combines housing, food, transport, and personal costs alongside tuition and fees.[web:28][web:31]

For this demo, the state baselines are set using representative ranges for room, board, transportation, and personal expenses reported in public cost-of-attendance and affordability research, not the official numbers for any school.[web:30][web:37]

Sliders for spending, takeout, bars, and partying adjust the baseline up or down to reflect different lifestyles. The result is an approximate yearly spending‑money target, not a guarantee of your actual costs.[web:27][web:29]

This CashPrep calculator runs as a static web page and is not built to collect personal information such as your name, address, or payment details. The numbers you type and the sliders you use are processed only in your browser to show you an estimate.[web:32][web:38]
